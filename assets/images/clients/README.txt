Client Logos Upload Guide

Upload your client logos in this folder.

Recommended naming:
- client-01.png
- client-02.png
- client-03.png
- client-04.png
- client-05.png
- client-06.png
- client-07.png
- client-08.png
- client-09.png

Preferred format:
- PNG with transparent background
- SVG also works
- Recommended max width: 320px
- Recommended max height: 160px

After uploading logos, open clients.html and update the client names/captions if needed.
