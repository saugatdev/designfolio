How AI is Changing Graphic Design & Photography

In this blog, we’ll explore how AI can be used to create stunning fashion and lifestyle photography, just like the visuals I made for Women’s Day, and how you can use AI tools to build a career or side hustle in design.

How AI is Changing Graphic Design & Photography: A Creative Revolution

08, Mar 2025 AI Design

A few weeks ago, I had this crazy idea—I wanted to create high-fashion photography for Women’s Day, but without hiring models, booking a studio, or spending hours editing. Sounds impossible, right?

That’s when I stumbled upon Google Image FX, an AI tool that lets you create hyper-realistic images just by describing them in words. I had seen AI-generated art before, but I never thought it could be used for fashion photography—until I tried it myself.

And let me tell you… it blew my mind.

In just minutes, I had professional-looking images that looked like they came straight out of a Prada campaign. The best part? I didn’t need a camera, lighting setup, or expensive software. Just AI, my imagination, and the right prompts.

Now, I’m going to show you exactly how I did it, so you can use AI for your own creative projects—whether you're a designer, photographer, or just someone who loves cool visuals.

Step 1: Have a Concept in Mind

Before jumping into AI tools, you need a vision for your project.

For Women’s Day, I wanted my visuals to show confidence, power, and elegance in different styles:

  • A laid-back summer vibe (a woman lounging by the pool, radiating effortless confidence).
  • A vintage elegance theme (models in classic outfits, set against historical architecture).
  • A modern streetwear attitude (a stylish woman in oversized casual wear, exuding coolness).

Think about what kind of mood and story you want to tell with your images. AI can create anything, but it helps if you know what you’re looking for.

Step 2: Use AI to Generate Your Images

I used Google Image FX to bring my ideas to life. It works by taking text descriptions (called prompts) and turning them into realistic photos.

Example Prompt I Used:

"A stylish woman lounging on a yellow poolside chair, wearing a striped swimsuit and yellow-tinted sunglasses, sipping a citrus drink. High fashion editorial, summer aesthetic, bright colors, cinematic lighting.”

And just like that, AI generated a stunning, magazine-worthy image.
